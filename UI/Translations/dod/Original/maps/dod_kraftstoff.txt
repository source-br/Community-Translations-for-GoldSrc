DOD_KRAFTSTOFF by Chris "Unreal" Scott (unreal@gamer.net.nz)

October, 1944, Deep in Allied territory

German forces, on a major offensive, have discovered an Allied 
fuel dump.  Low on fuel for their tanks, the Axis troops decide
to capture the town's supplies.

Allied Objectives: 
   - Defend the Fuel Truck
   - Defend the Fuel Dump

Axis Objectives:
   - Capture the Fuel Truck
   - Capture the Fuel Dump

Textures: Adam "Telnets" Grebinsky
