DOD_ZALEC by Patrick "Mojo" Krefting (mojo@theposse.org)

January 15, 1945, Western Front

Paratroopers have landed near the city of Zalec with the 
objective of capturing the town and removing all German 
resistance.

Allied Objectives:
   - Capture the fountain near the clock tower
   - Destroy the three Axis Panzers
   - Capture the Zalec church

Axis Objectives:
   - Defend the fountain
   - If the fountain is captured, fall back to the Panzers
   - If the tanks are destroyed, fall back to the church

Textures: Chad "Masako" Barnsdale, Daniel "Kleinluka" Luka, 
Scott "Kamikazi!" Jordan
